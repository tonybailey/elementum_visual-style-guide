Elementum Logo

A quick overview of our mark and how we use it

 

The Mark and the Lockup

Our mark is built entirely out of the Elementum Web, a framework using 4 concentric circles. The Elementum Web is compiled of 8 sectors, each consists of 4 cells, totaling 32 cells within the complete web.
